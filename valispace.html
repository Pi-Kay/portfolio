Aerospace · Traceability Design

Translating fragmented workflows into specifications for systems engineers

March 2021 – April 2023 · Valispace → Altium 365

Role

Sole designer, working closely with engineers. Surfaced hidden constraints in digital and offline workflows — regulatory logic, verification dependencies, traceability gaps — and translated them into design prototypes.

Also contributed to API design consistency and product documentation — reviewed interfaces, flagged inconsistencies, and gave feedback on clarity and annotations.

Design artifacts not shown due to post-acquisition IP. Public product documentation linked throughout.

Method

Aerospace SMEs rarely articulate the constraints they've internalized over decades. Much of the work was surfacing assumptions experts no longer questioned, then translating fragmented feature requests into coherent system flows.

Writing detailed user stories — specifying for a novice what an expert does by reflex — often fell to design rather than to the experts themselves. Holding the whole alongside the parts, across Master-Follower, V&V Matrix, and Review Center, meant designing for coherence at the system level while each screen stayed usable on its own.
