About

I've worked as a non-designer inside a highly design-mature company and as the only designer in an engineering-led one, often operating between engineers and users. That taught me how each side thinks, and where intention diverges from real-world use.

At Valispace (acquired by Altium 365), I joined as the first designer in an aerospace systems engineering company, evolving the design practice in context and iterating fast with engineers and domain experts. I contributed to features that helped secure a six-figure contract, now part of Altium 365.

At Apple, four years supporting Logic Pro and Final Cut Pro users across Europe gave me a close view of how design and engineering come together, and where complex tools break down in real-world edge cases. Current projects on the portfolio.
