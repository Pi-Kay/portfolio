Case 3

Health Hack - App Innovation

HACK&CON at National Health Insurance Conference

Date

07.-09.09.2022 in Leipzig, Germany

Use Case submitted by AOK, solved with health insurance employees

Tools

Pen & Paper, Figma, Keynote

Collaborators, Context

As sole designer among 5 health insurance professionals, I directed our AOK project toward conceptual innovation, leveraging my startup experience for time management and my systems thinking background to navigate healthcare regulations.

Process

[Detailed process description]

Explanation / Prompt / Learning

No AI used, only humans and simple drawing on paper and digitally in Figma pre-Ai - presented with Keynote. I recognized how I could apply my startup experience and help guide the team through the process in a short timeframe (3 days total) to come up with a concept and presentation.

Impacts, Results, Follow up

Our team won 2000 EUR prize 'most innovative Idea' competing with 250 participants at the Hackathon. Most unique was the support for individual family members of patients, not as family coach. Based on legal limitations as well as additional psychological burden fro all family members (systems awareness) and to counteract helper syndrome, exhaustion and projection (systems dynamics).
