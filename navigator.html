Interface Navigator

Problem

AI compliance in music is fragmented across legal documents, technical specs, and evolving regulations. Current resources are either too legal (inaccessible) or too simplified (incomplete).

Solution

A decision-tree tool that works bidirectionally: artists can check if their work was used without consent, and companies can verify their AI pipeline is ethical. Input a use case, get requirements, risks, and action steps.

Use Cases

  • Consent & Attribution — Was this work used with permission?
  • Training Data Provenance — Where did training data come from?
  • Generated Output Rights — Who owns AI-generated work?
  • Transparency & Disclosure — When must I disclose AI use?
